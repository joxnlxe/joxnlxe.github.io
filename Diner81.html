Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery?

We currently offer take-out only. We are working on delivery services so please stay tuned!

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes! We have several vegetarian-friendly dishes such as Bibimbap (without meat), Japchae, and various sushi rolls. Feel free to ask our staff for recommendations.

Can I make a reservation?

We accept reservations for groups and special events. Please call us at +1 (506) 466-2223 to book a table.

Do you offer gluten-free dishes?

Yes, some of our dishes are gluten-free. Ask our staff for details when you order.

Which days do you serve Korean & Japanese dishes?

Korean dishes are available from Wednesday to Sunday, and Japanese dishes from Thursday to Saturday.
